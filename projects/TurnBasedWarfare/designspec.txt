Fantasy Auto-Battler Unit Roster
================================

CORE UNITS (Tier 1 Base → Tier 2 Specs)
---------------------------------------

Watchman (Tank)
  Base: Shield Bash — reduce damage briefly
  • Bulwark — Shield Wall (block ranged)
  • Commander — Rallying Cry (+atk/move)
  • Vindicator — Vengeance Slam (AoE scales with fallen allies)

Hunter (Ranged DPS)
  Base: Piercing Shot — every 4th arrow pierces
  • Ranger — Deadeye (crit + pierce)
  • Marksman — Headshot (snipe lowest HP)
  • Beastmaster — Wild Companion (summon pet)

Apprentice (Caster)
  Base: Magic Bolt — simple ranged spell
  • Fire Mage — Inferno Wave (fire line AoE)
  • Ice Mage — Absolute Zero (freeze group)
  • Arcanist — Mana Infusion (restore ally mana)

Rogue (Assassin/Utility)
  Base: Backstab — bonus damage from behind
  • Assassin — Shadow Dance (untargetable burst)
  • Poisoner — Venom Nova (DoT poison)
  • Trickster — Mirage (illusion clones)

Acolyte (Healer)
  Base: Heal — restore lowest ally HP
  • Cleric — Sanctuary (protective dome)
  • Zealot — Purge Flame (AoE burn + heal allies)
  • Oracle — Divine Inspiration (restore team mana)

Slinger (Light AoE Ranged)
  Base: Stone Throw — splash dmg
  • Bomber — Cluster Bomb (big AoE)
  • Fire Slinger — Burning Pitch (ground fire DoT)
  • Ricochet — Chain Shot (bouncing attack)

Spearman (Anti-Cavalry)
  Base: Pike Thrust — bonus vs charges
  • Phalanx — Spear Wall (stop charge)
  • Dragoon — Storm of Spears (volley throw)
  • Temple Guard — Sacred Spear (+armor aura)

Warhounds (Beast Swarm)
  Base: Pack Instinct — stronger near allies
  • Alpha Wolf — Pack Hunt (focus fire)
  • Dire Wolves — Howl of Terror (slow atk speed)
  • Spirit Wolves — Ethereal Howl (magic dmg + invuln)

Occultist (Dark Debuffer)
  Base: Hex Bolt — weaken enemy atk
  • Hexer — Mass Hex (polymorph group)
  • Cultist — Blood Pact (sac HP to heal allies)
  • Plaguebearer — Pestilence (contagious DoT)

Thralls (Disposable Swarm)
  Base: Pike Stab — weak melee
  • Shieldbearer — Turtle Shell (invuln at low HP)
  • Martyr — Last Stand (explode on death)
  • Berserker Mob — Blood Surge (death → atk speed buff)

Knight (Heavy Cavalry)
  Base: Charge — rush + impact dmg
  • Crusader — Holy Charge (heal allies while charging)
  • Cataphract — Iron Charge (unstoppable)
  • Dark Knight — Soul Rend (lifedrain)

Ranger (Advanced Archer)
  Base: Volley Fire — multi-shot
  • Sharpshooter — Perfect Shot (massive hit)
  • Beastmaster — Call of the Hawk (pet summon)
  • Trapper — Root Net (immobilize foes)

Elementalist (Tri-Mage)
  Base: Elemental Bolt — rotates fire/ice/lightning
  • Pyromancer — Inferno (firestorm)
  • Cryomancer — Blizzard (freeze AoE)
  • Stormcaller — Chain Tempest (mobile lightning storm)

Assassin (Stealth Melee)
  Base: Ambush — big opener
  • Shadowdancer — Vanish (AoE re-engage)
  • Venomblade — Toxic Bloom (poison burst)
  • Nightstalker — Dread Assault (fear enemies)

Druid (Nature Support)
  Base: Minor Heal + Treant Sprout
  • Grovekeeper — Nature’s Renewal (heal all)
  • Beast Shaman — Wild Stampede (summon beasts)
  • Corrupted Druid — Rotting Growth (poison vines)

Ballista (Anti-Large Artillery)
  Base: Piercing Bolt — big dmg vs large foes
  • Siegebreaker — Titan Killer (%HP dmg)
  • Storm Launcher — Thunder Bolt (stun)
  • Dragon Slayer — Sky Piercer (delete flier)

Shaman (Totem Mage)
  Base: Totem Drop — buff/debuff
  • Spirit Caller — Ancestors’ Wrath (ghost allies)
  • Earthshaker — Earthquake Totem (big stun)
  • Stormbringer — Totemic Storm (lightning chain)

Berserker (Bruiser)
  Base: Rage — grows stronger at low HP
  • Ravager — Blood Frenzy (double atk + lifesteal)
  • Warbringer — Battle Shout (team buff)
  • Executioner — Headsman’s Strike (execute low HP)

Warlock (Dark Caster)
  Base: Dark Bolt — ranged shadow attack
  • Curseweaver — Mass Hex (multi-polymorph)
  • Pyromancer — Inferno Blast (meteors)
  • Blood Mage — Blood Rain (sac HP → AoE dmg)

Treant (Nature Tank)
  Base: Root Strike — slows enemy
  • Ancient Guardian — Entangling Roots (AoE root)
  • Grovebound — Rebirth (revives once)
  • Corrupted Treant — Plague Spores (death cloud)
